Cosmic Encounter

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Fantasy Flight Games
  • Year Released: 2008

Description

Cosmic Encounter is a 3-8 player board game where each player becomes the leader of one of dozens of alien races, each with its own unique power. On a player's turn, they become the offense, and encounter another player on a planet in an attempt to create a foreign colony. The winner(s) are the first player(s) to have five colonies on any planets outside their home system. The players must use force, cunning, and diplomacy to ensure their victory.

Game Categories

  • Genre: Sci-Fi Adventure
  • Players: 3-8
  • Play Time: 60-120 minutes
